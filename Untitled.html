Slow Motion

 

Our player makes it easy to watch our videos and learn on your time. If Mike's fingers are moving faster than the speed of lightning, try clicking the Slow-Motion button. This will allow you to slow it down and work out those fingers.

Replay


Click the Replay button once to rewind 10 seconds. Double-clicking will allow you to loop those ten seconds until you get the riff. A triple click will allow you to loop the video longer than you can possibly imagine.

Chapter Select


Many of our free videos and all of our Premium Membership catalogue contains bookmarks to each section of the song, so you can quickly find the verse, chorus, bridge or other areas you want to learn

 

Zoom!


Need to get really close to see an intricate phrase or finger movement?  That's where the Zoom function comes in handy.  To exit, simply click anywhere outside of the zoomed area.
